# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Build a beginner-friendly student study app with planner, Pomodoro timer, notes, flashcards, exam countdown, progress tracking, and an AI study assistant.

**Planned changes:**
- Implement Daily Study Planner with subject management and timetable view
- Create Pomodoro Focus Timer with 25-minute sessions, 5-minute breaks, and motivational messages
- Build Notes Section with subject-based organization and editing capabilities
- Implement Flashcards Mode with question/answer cards and swipe navigation
- Add Exam Countdown feature showing days remaining for upcoming exams
- Integrate AI Study Assistant chatbot for explaining topics in simple language
- Create Progress Tracker displaying study streak and daily hours studied
- Design minimal, light-themed UI with soft colors, large buttons, and mobile-responsive layout

**User-visible outcome:** Students can plan their study schedule, use a focus timer, take notes organized by subject, review flashcards, track exam deadlines, monitor their study progress with streaks and hours logged, and get help from a friendly AI assistant that explains concepts in easy-to-understand languageâ€”all in a clean, beginner-friendly interface optimized for mobile devices.
